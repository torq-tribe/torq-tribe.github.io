Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Torq Tribe ("we," "our," or "us"), we believe your health data is personal. Our architecture is designed to prioritize on-device processing for AI features, minimizing the amount of data that leaves your phone.

1. Data Processing & AI

On-Device Intelligence: Torq Tribe uses Apple's on-device models (Apple Intelligence) to generate workout plans and analyze your fitness data. This means the sensitive processing of your biomechanics and schedule happens directly on your iPhone, not on our servers.

We do not use your personal workout data to train our own proprietary LLMs in the cloud.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the gamified fitness experience:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication details (via Supabase).
  • Health & Fitness Data: Workout logs, exercises performed, body metrics (weight, height), and injuries. This data is stored in our secure database to allow you to sync across devices and compete on leaderboards.
  • Gamification Data: "Embers" earned, streaks, and Caveman status.

3. Apple HealthKit Integration

Torq Tribe integrates with Apple’s HealthKit to write workout data (calories, duration) and read biometric data (weight) to improve plan accuracy.

  • We do not sell HealthKit data to third parties.
  • We do not use HealthKit data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage & Third Parties

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase: Used for secure cloud database storage and authentication.
  • YouTube API: Used to display exercise demonstration videos. By using these videos, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete Your Account: You can request full account deletion directly within the app settings. This will remove your data from our servers.
  • Export Data: You may request a copy of your workout history.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@torqtribe.com.
