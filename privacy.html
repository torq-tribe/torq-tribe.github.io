Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Torq Tribe (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile app and related services (the “Service”).

Fitness / Health Disclaimer (Important):

Torq Tribe provides general fitness and educational information only. It is not medical advice and is not a substitute for professional guidance. Consult a qualified healthcare provider before beginning any new exercise program. Stop immediately if you experience pain, dizziness, faintness, chest pain, or shortness of breath, and seek medical attention as appropriate. In an emergency, call your local emergency number.

1) Information we collect

We collect information in three ways: (a) information you provide, (b) information generated by your use, and (c) third-party data if you choose to connect it (future).

1A) Information you provide

  • Account information: When you sign up/sign in, we use Supabase Auth to authenticate your account (e.g., email and auth identifiers/tokens used for login).
  • Onboarding/profile information: You may enter fitness profile details such as goals, preferences, experience, schedule, available equipment, session length, injuries, and related inputs to personalize your plan.
  • Support communications: If you email us, we receive the information you include in your message.

1B) Information generated by your use

  • Workout plan and activity: We generate and retrieve your workout plan and may store workout progress/history to provide the Service.
  • On-device caching: The app may store limited data locally on your device to improve performance and reliability (for example, cached workout history).
  • Operational logs: Like most services, infrastructure providers may process basic technical logs (e.g., timestamps, IP address, request metadata) for security and service operation.

1C) Third-party connections (planned)

  • Apple Health (planned): If added, you will control whether Torq Tribe can read/write specific Apple Health data. We will update this policy before launching this feature.
  • Analytics (planned): If added, we will disclose what data is collected, how it is used, and any available choices/controls before enabling analytics.

2) How we use your information

  • Provide and operate the Service (authentication, profile storage, plan generation, and progress tracking).
  • Personalize workouts based on your inputs (e.g., goals, equipment, injuries, preferences).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, debug issues, and improve reliability.
  • Respond to your support requests and communicate important updates.

3) How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the Service:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase for authentication and database services. Your account and profile data are stored/processed via these services as needed to provide the app.
  • Third-party links: The app may include links to third-party content (e.g., tutorials). When you open a third-party link, that third party’s policies apply.
  • Legal/safety: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

4) Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Some information may remain in backups for a limited period.

5) Your choices and requests

  • Update info: You may update certain profile information within the app where available.
  • Deletion request: To request account/data deletion, email torqtribe@icloud.com from the email associated with your account.
  • Device data: Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device, but server-stored data remains until deleted per your request.

6) Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7) Children’s privacy

Torq Tribe is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and update the effective date above.

9) Contact

Privacy questions and requests: torqtribe@icloud.com
